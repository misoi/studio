WHO WE ARE

Moris' studio is a fully equipped recording studio offering an ideal recording environment for bands and groups looking to record a demo, mixtape, single or an album. The studio offers a digital recording environment with a 56-channel mixing desk and is based around a Mac Pro music system. In addition, the studio offers a wide range of quality microphones, professional outboard equipment, virtual instruments and plugins. Our studio includes a control room, live room, drum room and vocal booth. Our objective is to make your recording studio time as enjoyable, comfortable and professional as possible. Whatever your recording requirements, Select Recording Studios has the equipment and expertise you need for your recording projects. Coupled with the talented music producers and sound engineers, we have had over 20 years experience in recording, mixing and songwriting in a wide variety of musical styles, including Hip-Hop, R'n'B, Rock/Metal, Dance, Indie, Electro, Pop, Jazz and Classical and musical theatre
