WHO WE ARE

Moris' studio is a fully equipped recording studio offering an ideal recording environment for bands and groups looking to record a demo, mixtape, single or an album. The studio offers a digital recording environment with a 56-channel mixing desk and is based around a Mac Pro music system.

Coupled with the talented music producers and sound engineers, we have had over 20 years experience in recording, mixing and songwriting in a wide variety of musical styles, including Hip-Hop, R'n'B, Rock/Metal, Dance, Indie, Electro, Pop, Jazz, Gospel and Classical and musical theatre
